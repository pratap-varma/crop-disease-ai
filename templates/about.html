About CropDiseaseAI

Bridging the gap between cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence and local agricultural communities.

⚠️ Problem Statement

Crop leaf diseases present a severe threat to global food security and farming livelihoods. Traditional diagnostic methods depend heavily on agricultural experts, which is often slow, expensive, and unavailable in remote regions. Delayed diagnosis results in uncontrolled infection spread, crop loss, and high chemical remedy dependency, impacting smallholder farmers most.

🎯 Our Objectives

  • ✔ Instant Diagnosis: Deliver immediate AI-based leaf disease identification.
  • ✔ Integrated Remedy Guide: Suggest organic and chemical solutions.
  • ✔ Multilingual Access: Enable dynamic translations in English, Hindi, and Telugu.
  • ✔ Modern Awareness: Promote sustainable soil, water, and weather practices.

Technologies We Used

Tailwind CSS

Responsive components and dynamic visual grid layouts.

Python & Flask

Backend application logic, dynamic routing, and session controls.

Gemini AI API

Google's vision models for foliage disease diagnosis reports.

Google Translate

Instant translations supporting English, Hindi, and Telugu.
